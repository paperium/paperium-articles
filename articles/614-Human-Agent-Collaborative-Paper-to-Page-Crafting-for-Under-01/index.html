The authors address the challenges researchers face in manually generating these webpages, proposing a coarse-to-fine pipeline that integrates AI-driven content generation with human oversight. The system incorporates dedicated agents to mitigate AI hallucination and ensure the accuracy of the output. Additionally, the authors introduce PageBench, a benchmark for evaluating the performance of this new task. Experimental results demonstrate that AutoPage can produce high-quality, visually appealing webpages efficiently and cost-effectively.
