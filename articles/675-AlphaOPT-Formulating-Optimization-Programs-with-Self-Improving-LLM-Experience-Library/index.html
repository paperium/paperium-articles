Overview of AlphaOPT: Advancing LLM-Driven Optimization Modeling

This article introduces AlphaOPT, an innovative self-improving experience library designed to enhance Large Language Models' (LLMs) capabilities in optimization modeling. It addresses the challenge of automating the translation of informal language into precise mathematical formulations and executable solver code, where prior LLM approaches struggled with brittle prompting or costly retraining. AlphaOPT operates through a continuous two-phase cycle: Library Learning extracts solver-verified, structured insights from failures, while Library Evolution refines their applicability for improved transfer. This enables LLMs to learn efficiently from limited demonstrations, even answers alone, without parameter updates. The research demonstrates AlphaOPT's steady improvement with more data and its superior out-of-distribution generalization.
